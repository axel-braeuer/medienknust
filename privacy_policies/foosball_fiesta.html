PRIVACY POLICY - Foosball Fiesta: Axel's Ed.

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Foosball Fiesta: Axel's Ed. for mobile devices, created by Axel Bräuer.

The Application is a casual game, a virtual table soccer game, suitable for single-player and 2-player modes on the same device.

AUTOMATICALLY COLLECTED INFORMATION:
The developer does not collect or store personal information, however, since the application is built with Unity, please note, that
for example technical information about your device may be sent to Unity for their purposes.

It is advised to look at Unity´s privacy policy as well under following link: https://unity.com/de/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy.

Also Unity´s FAQ page regarding their privacy policy at: https://unity.com/de/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-faq is very helpful.

DOES THE DEVELOPER COLLECT PRECISE REAL-TIME LOCATION INFORMATION OF THE DEVICE?
The developer does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device.

DO THIRD PARTIES SEE AND/OR HAVE ACCESS TO INFORMATION OBTAINED BY THE APPLICATION?
Again, please look up Unity´s privacy policy.
